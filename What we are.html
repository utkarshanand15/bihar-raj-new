MAIN OBJECTIVE

"Bihar Raj" A foundation that is full of Energy, focusing on unbaised agendas and public awareness.

"BIHAR RAJ" is a digital media platform . This is an initiative taken by youth of Bihar. It's aim is to give voice to the grassroots issues of Bihar, which have gone away from the mainstream in the corporate world It is our commitment to be completely impartial. Also the common man should become its cornerstone.And through ideological empowerment, social revolution can be started till the last ends of the society.

OUR GOALS

  • Bihar Raj's goal isn't to follow every trends just to gain some likes and followers.
  • There is no connection with active politics, This is the aim to make the system better.
  • The purpose is to give place to every ground related issues concerning Bihar.
  • Bihar Raj stands for what is right even if thats means standing alone
    just to inspire people to know the facts and to spread social awareness.
